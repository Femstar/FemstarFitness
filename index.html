Who I Am

I'm a certified personal trainer and have been training clients for over 15 years in New Jersey and New York. My programs are created to meet the individual needs of my clients and include nutrition, cross training, and custom exercise design. I'm motivated by the positive change I bring to my clients. My clients' success = My success!

Benefits

I help clients identify realistic and rewarding goals. It is my expertise that allows me to target specific areas/needs by designing customized workout plans.

During your workouts, my job is to observe/correct your form for maximum results - pushing you just far enough beyond your comfort zone without adding risk of injury or discomfort. My goal is to keep you motivated and accountable, while having you end each session with a great sense of accomplishment.
